ANCBuddy is a Bose QC Ultra Mac app for daily menu-bar control: switch Quiet, Aware, and Immersion, check status, and use optional AI Auto-EQ without reaching for your phone.

## Built for the QC Ultra line

ANCBuddy supports:

- Bose QuietComfort Ultra Headphones Gen 1
- Bose QuietComfort Ultra Headphones Gen 2
- Bose QuietComfort Ultra Earbuds 2nd Gen

It is not a generic Bluetooth utility for every headphone brand. It is focused on compatible Bose QC Ultra hardware.

## Daily Mac controls

The core workflow is simple: pair the headphones with your Mac, open ANCBuddy from the menu bar, and choose Quiet, Aware, or Immersion. Battery and connection status stay visible in the same panel.
