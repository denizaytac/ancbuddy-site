ANCBuddy is an independent Mac alternative for daily Bose QC Ultra controls: it puts listening modes, battery status, and optional AI Auto-EQ in the macOS menu bar.

## What Bose documents

Bose documents the Bose app for iOS and Android devices in [Bose Support](https://support.bose.com/s/article/qc-headphonearn-download-the-bose-app?language=en_US). ANCBuddy does not claim to be Bose software and does not replace every Bose app feature.

## What ANCBuddy is good for

ANCBuddy focuses on the repetitive Mac-desk workflow:

- Switch Quiet, Aware, and Immersion without opening a phone app
- See battery and connection status while working
- Use optional AI Auto-EQ for a track-aware 3-band EQ profile

For firmware updates and deeper product setup, keep the Bose phone app installed.

## Independent by design

ANCBuddy is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Bose Corporation. It uses Bose names only to describe compatible hardware.
