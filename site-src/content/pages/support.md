ANCBuddy support is handled directly by the developer for setup, Bose QC Ultra connection issues, update questions, and AI Auto-EQ questions.

## Contact

Email [denoaytac62@gmail.com](mailto:denoaytac62@gmail.com).

For faster support, include:

- Mac model and macOS version
- ANCBuddy version
- Bose device model
- Whether the headphones or earbuds are already paired in macOS Bluetooth settings
- Whether regular Mac audio playback works
