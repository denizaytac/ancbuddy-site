ANCBuddy supports Bose QC Ultra Earbuds 2nd Gen on Mac, with menu-bar controls for Quiet, Aware, Immersion, battery status, and optional AI Auto-EQ.

## Earbuds workflow

Pair the earbuds with macOS Bluetooth, then use ANCBuddy from the menu bar for daily listening-mode changes. For product setup and firmware updates, keep the Bose phone app available.

## What ANCBuddy adds

- Direct mode switching from your Mac
- Battery and connection status in one panel
- Optional AI Auto-EQ for the current track
- A small native utility instead of a phone-app detour
