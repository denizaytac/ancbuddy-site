ANCBuddy shows Bose QC Ultra battery and connection status in a Mac menu-bar panel, so you do not have to depend only on generic macOS Bluetooth indicators.

## Battery visibility in context

macOS handles Bluetooth pairing and audio device connection. Apple documents the standard Bluetooth flow in [Apple Support](https://support.apple.com/guide/mac-help/connect-a-wireless-accessory-blth1004/mac). For QC Ultra users, ANCBuddy adds a product-specific panel next to the listening mode controls.

## What you see in ANCBuddy

The ANCBuddy panel shows:

- Connected device name
- Battery status
- Bluetooth connection status
- Quiet, Aware, and Immersion controls

For setup help, see [troubleshooting](/troubleshooting.html) or [support](/support.html).
