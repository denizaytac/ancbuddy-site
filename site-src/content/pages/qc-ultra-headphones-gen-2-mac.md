ANCBuddy supports Bose QC Ultra Headphones Gen 2 on macOS, including menu-bar switching for Quiet, Aware, and Immersion.

## Supported controls

With Gen 2 headphones, ANCBuddy focuses on the same daily controls as the rest of the QC Ultra line:

- Listening mode switching
- Battery and connection status
- Optional AI Auto-EQ
- Launch at Login
- Built-in update checks

Install the current release from the [download page](/download.html). If the headphones are paired but do not appear, use the [troubleshooting guide](/troubleshooting.html).
